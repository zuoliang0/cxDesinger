# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make participation in this project a respectful and productive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on the technical issue and project goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or sensitive user data.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.
